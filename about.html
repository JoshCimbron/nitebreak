Meet Josh

Designer at Wongdoody x Infosys

As an Interactive designer, I have a passion for discovering simple solutions to complex problems. I've worked with small businesses to create more accessible and visually stunning websites that thoughtfully balance customer and business needs.

Before I discovered design I worked as a store manager in the health and wellness space. I was an integral part of the user experience for thousands of customers, I just didn't know it yet! I believe that a little empathy goes a long way in creating solutions that have a lasting impact.

When I’m not slinging some wireframes I enjoy casting spells with my DND group, sketching, and watching spooky movies.
