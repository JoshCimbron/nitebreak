4 Seasons

The best pan asian cuisine in providence

The Challenge

I was entrusted with designing and developing a new website for 4 Seasons. 4 Seasons serves up yummy pan asian cuisine in a family friendly space. Over 11,000 hungry customers visit their website every month.

Check out the website

Identifying user goals and needs

I worked closely with the owners and conducted research to learn more about our users. We identified their goals, what they need to accomplish those goals, and any constraints preventing them from accomplishing those goals.

Finding the right aesthetic

I created two different moodboards to guide the site’s aesthetic. We decided that a balanced color pallete was the best representation of the qualities that make 4 Seasons unique. A place where people can find peace and excitement from good food and company.

I created moodboards to explore different aesthetic themes. I worked closely with the owners to identify elements that worked well with the 4 Season’s brand.

Style guides helped keep the website consistent and easy to read.
