4 Seasons

The best pan asian cuisine in providence

The Challenge

I was entrusted with designing and developing a new website for 4 Seasons. 4 Seasons serves up yummy pan asian cuisine in a family friendly space. Over 11,000 hungry customers visit their website every month. Now that's a lot of mouths to feed!

Check out the website

Identifying user goals and needs

You can’t make a great website if you design for the wrong user. That’s why we always start our projects by learning more about our guests. We identify their goals, the tools they need to accomplish them, and any possible problems they may encounter.

Finding the right aesthetic

4 Seasons is more than just a restaurant. Between good food and great company, it’s where you find your happy place. We worked closely with the owners to embody the qualities that make 4 Seasons unique. Research, moodboards, and wireframing helped us explore different directions for the site.

We created two moodboards to explore different aesthetic themes. We worked closely with the owners to identify elements that worked well with the 4 Season’s brand.

Style guides helped keep the website consistent and easy to read.

Exploring directions for the structure of the website with low fidelity wireframes.

Finalizing the visual direction with high fidelity wireframes.

Ready. Set. Launch.
