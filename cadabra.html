Cadabra

Bring stories to life with magical sound effects

The Challenge

Bedtime stories just got a little more magical. Cadabra is a personal project that makes reading more immersive and fun. It allows adults to customize stories for their kids with awesome sound effects that play as you read along. Choose sound effects from an extensive library or create your own. The end goal for this project is to create a convincing prototype.

Identifying user goals and needs

Cadabra’s demographic is geared towards new readers and their guardians. We use empathy mapping to consolidate our research and assumptions about the user. This diagram determines what our user’s goals are, the tools they need to accomplish these goals, and any roadblocks they might face. That way we can create awesome experiences tailored just for them!

From Blueprint to Prototype

Designing an app like Cadabra can get complicated fast. We prioritize the most important features that we can design and build within a sprint cycle. We begin our workflow by mapping out the information architecture. Then, we work on the visual direction of the app. Finally, we create a working prototype suitable for observational studies.

A user flow is a great tool to map out the app’s structure. The user flow helps us prioritize the most important screens to design and build for our prototype.

We begin to flesh out the most important structural elements of the interface with lo-fi wireframes.

A moodboard helps keep the visual direction of the project consistent and focused.

We make things pop by incorporating colors, font families, and other stylistic choices to build high fidelity wireframes.

Get Creating Fast

Your time should be spent on exploring new worlds with your favorite little ones, not in learning complicated software. Cadabra makes creation fast and easy so you can focus on the things that really matter.

Explore an extensive library of books and save your favorites to read and customize.

A simple menu and intuitive touch controls makes finding and adding sound effects easy.

Can't find a sound effect? Record and save your own.

Everyone needs a little help sometimes. A short and engaging tutorial helps new users customize their first story.

What we learned

Building a convincing prototype is tough work. We overshot our projected deadline by working on too many problems and features in one sprint cycle. We decided to spend one more week augmenting Cadabra's creation tool. Based on user feedback We created a short tutorial to help new user's start creating content quickly and confidently. Finally, we had a little time to iterate any usability concerns we discovered.
