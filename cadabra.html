Cadabra

Bring stories to life with magical sound effects

The Challenge

Bedtime stories just got a little more magical. Cadabra is a personal project that makes reading more immersive and fun. It allows adults to customize stories for their kids with awesome sound effects that play along as you read. Choose sound effects from an extensive library or create your own. The end goal for this project is to create a convincing prototype.

Identifying user goals and needs

Cadabra’s demographic is geared towards new readers and their guardians. I used empathy mapping to consolidate my research and assumptions about the user. This diagram determines what the user’s goals are, the tools they need to accomplish those goals, and any roadblocks they might face. Ultimately, I decided to focus on crafting an experience for adults. More specifically, adults that need to customize stories quickly before bedtime.

PoV Problem Statement

I need a quick way to customize books with sound effects for my children’s bedtime stories. I’m short on time because I’m busy with work, errands, and taking care of the kids.

From Blueprint to Prototype

Designing an app like Cadabra can get complicated fast. I prioritize the most important features that I can design and build within a sprint cycle. I begin my workflow by mapping out the information architecture. Then, I work on the visual direction of the app. Finally, I create a working prototype suitable for observational studies.

A user flow is a great tool to map out the app’s structure. The user flow helps us prioritize the most important screens to design and build for our prototype.

I begin to flesh out the most important structural elements of the interface with lo-fi wireframes.

A moodboard helps keep the visual direction of the project consistent and focused.

I make things pop by incorporating colors, font families, and other stylistic choices to build high fidelity wireframes.

Get Creating Fast

The primary feature I focused on was the creation tool for customizing stories. It's vital that adults can customize stories quickly so they can focus on the things that really matter, spending quality time with their kids.

Explore an extensive library of books and save your favorites to read and customize.

A simple menu and intuitive touch controls makes finding and adding sound effects easy.

Can't find a sound effect? Record and save your own.

Everyone needs a little help sometimes. A short and engaging tutorial helps new users customize their first story.

What I learned

The complexity and scale of this project highlighted the importance of project scoping and time management. It emphasized the value of keeping a backlog to prioritize important tasks and avoid feature creep.
